# Feedback Room — Round 2 (Raw Persona Responses)

> Date: 2026-06-24 · Product state: current build (DIRECT charges, contractor = merchant of record, ~8.4% all-in now visible; SMS reminders, Smart Business Card + QR + social assets, CSV export, reschedule, per-client pricing, branded receipts all SHIPPED).
> Panel: 13 personas, independent. Format: Verdict · Pay? · What's-now-there · Fee reaction (~8.4%) · Top-3 (F#) · Friction · Churn · Quote.

---

## CORE / LOW-END

**Marco** (lawn, ES, $60) — **Conditional, warmer than R1.** Shipped SMS + QR + branded receipt + reschedule = "this is a product now, not a demo." Fee: $5.04 on a $60 card job; at $20k/mo = $1,680/mo → wants a **"pass fee to client" toggle** so he stops explaining a higher price. Top 3: **F20, F14 (deposits), F5**. Friction: explaining the 8.4% to a client every time. Churn: Stripe **payout delay** ("money not in my account till Tuesday"). Quote: *"It works — but if I'm charged 8% and have to explain the higher price to my client, the problem you solved created another one."*

**Eager newbie** (lawn, EN, new) — **Conditional.** QR + downloadable social images = "a glow-up, I'd print door-hangers Saturday." Referral card cool but "no tracked reward, so I won't push it." Fee fine now ($3.78/$45), scary at $400 jobs. Top 3: **F20, F14 (deposit + legit estimate), F5**. Friction: Stripe Connect SSN screen — wants a 20-sec "this is Stripe, your info never touches us" explainer. Churn: one payment goes sideways + no live support. Quote: *"The QR card goes on my truck this weekend — but make me send recurring invoices by hand and I'll forget half of them and cancel."*

**Joaquín** (pest, ES-only, anti-tech) — **Conditional (up from near-no).** Real Spanish + QR card pulled him in. Fee: seeing **TWO fees** feels like a "double bite — makes me feel like a fool; show ONE line: 8.4% and what I keep." Fee-flight certain on $300+ jobs. Top 3: **F20, F1 (must see it work), F14**. Friction: Stripe Connect bank screen — needs Spanish phone walk-through in Pro Setup. Churn: support not in Spanish / email-only. Quote: *"Put a Spanish-speaking person in front of me to explain the 8.4% with paper and pencil… maybe I give it a shot. Maybe."*

**Tyrell** (pressure wash, social) — **Conditional.** QR PNG + social story assets = "real time saved, converts followers." But card still can't close: **no deposit, no reviews, no before/after**. Fee ok now, "the second a competitor is 4.5% all-in I'm gone; the Early Adopter lock locks me into the HIGHER rate." Top 3: **F14 (deposit on the intake page), F12 (reviews on card), F11 (before/after)**. Friction: intake lead goes cold — no on-page booking/deposit. Churn: a competitor ships QR-intake + deposit + auto-review in one flow. Quote: *"The card looks pro enough that people click it — but if I can't take their deposit on that same page, I'm still texting Cash App links like it's 2021."*

**Linda** (window, ~8 clients, $18k/yr) — **Conditional, leaning walk-away.** CSV export + branded link moved her off "walk away"; QR "charming at my size." Fee: ~8.4% pushes her all-in toward **11–15% of card revenue**; check-payers stay checks → she's a low-fee customer. Top 3: **F1 (only ROI at her size), F20, F14**. Friction: dashboard built for $20k/mo shops; wants a "small operator mode." Churn: month-3 math ($86 fees on $1,100) → cancels quietly. Asks for a **sub-$20 lite tier**. Quote: *"It's clever, and a busier person would love it — I'm just not sure it loves me back at my size."*

## CROSS-CUTTING SIGNALS (Round 2)

1. **F14 deposits/quotes is now the #1 ask — 11/13.** Making the fee visible made everyone want money up front + signed estimates. Deposits double as anti-fee-flight (captured in-platform) and anti-ghost.
2. **Fee-flight intensified now that ~8.4% is visible.** Universal, quantified asks: a **"pass fee to client" surcharge toggle** (Marco), a **per-invoice cap** (Sasha $75, Brittany $300/mo), an **ACH/low-fee tier** (Sasha, Diego, Jenny), and **flat per-member fee on recurring agreements** (Charlie $1.50–2). Tyrell: "one competitor at 4.5% and I'm gone."
3. **F20 recurring auto-billing still gates the entire recurring half** (Jenny walk-away, Charlie binary, Rosa, Dave, Marco, Joaquín, Linda).
4. **New friction surfaced:** Stripe **payout speed** (Marco churn), Stripe Connect **onboarding explainer** still #1 friction for ES/new (Eager, Joaquín), and the **"two fees on the receipt" presentation** (Joaquín wants one combined line).
5. **Lawn-care positioning** still dents non-lawn trades (Charlie, Rosa, Marcus).
6. **Low-end pricing floor confirmed** (Linda) — needs a sub-$20 lite tier or deliberate exclusion.
